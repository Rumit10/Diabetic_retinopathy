About us

    We created a model, which represents a direction for automatic 
detection of Diabetic Retinopathy in FundusPhotographs. To predict the
severity of diabetic retinopathy we have implemented a method that is 
based on the classification of disease which is followed by feature 
extraction from the pixel level result and a machine learning method. 

Our method was performed on test data from the Kaggle competition.
Our method approaches the processing of Fundus Photographs with Neural 
Network and detecting the level of Diabetic Retinopathy in patients.
